Monthly Income

Monthly Income is a display of your income for each month of the year.

  • Your Total Payments, Total Deposits, Monthly Income, Average Income, and Overall Balance are displayed.
  • Select the year you wish to calculate and the totals will be displayed. The most recent year in your ledger is selected by default.
  • The Status for the selected year is also displayed. This lets you know whether your ledger was positive or negative for that year.
