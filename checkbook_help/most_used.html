Most Used Categories/Payees

Most Used Categories/Payees let's you see all your categories or payees and how many times they occur in your ledger. Below are the items that are displayed.

  • # Transactions: Counts how many times a category or payee occurs in your ledger showing the most used items.
  • Category or Payee: The category or payee that you have used in your ledger.
  • Total Payments: Displays the total payments for the category or payee based on either the selected year or your entire ledger.
  • Total Deposits: Displays the total deposits for the category or payee based on either the selected year or your entire ledger.
